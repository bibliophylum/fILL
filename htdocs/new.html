About fILL

fILL is the interlibrary loan management system for all Manitoba public libraries. It connects your local library to the millions of items available in Manitoba’s libraries.

If you are unable to find an item after searching your library’s catalogue you can use fILL to find it online and have it delivered to your public library.

fILL enables library patrons to quickly and easily search the catalogues of many Manitoba public libraries at the same time. When you request an item through fILL your local library staff will try to borrow the item from the lending library for you.

Creating an account

To use fILL you will need to create an account. All you need is a valid Manitoba public library card.

Creating an account is easy...

  1. From the Welcome page, click the "Library Patrons" button
  2. On the login page, click the "Sign up!" button
  3. Follow the instructions to select your home library
  4. Enter your name and library card number, and choose a username and password

When your account is created you will be able to log in and start using fILL.

Privacy

fILL does not keep a record of your search history nor does it keep a record of request transactions after borrowed items are returned. Logging into your account is necessary in order for your library to validate the request; some non-identifying data may be retained for statistical purposes.
