About fILL

fILL is the interlibrary loan management system for all Manitoba public libraries. It connects your local library to the millions of items available in Manitoba’s libraries.

If you are unable to find an item after searching your library’s catalogue you can use fILL to find it online and have it delivered to your local public library.

fILL enables library patrons like you to quickly and easily search the catalogues of many Manitoba public libraries at the same time. When you find the item that you'd like to request, fILL prepares the interlibrary loan request for your public library and the local library staff to try to borrow the item from the lending library for you.

Creating an account

You will need to create a fILL account to use the system, so that when you make a request for a book your library will know who the request is for.

Creating an accout is easy...

  1. From the Welcome page, click the button to log in
  2. On the login page, click the "Sign up!" button
  3. Follow the instructions to select your home library
  4. Choose a username and password

      5. Privacy

      fILL does not keep a record of your search history nor does it keep a record of request transactions after borrowed items are returned. Logging into your account is necessary in order for your library to validate the request; some non-identifying data may be retained for statistical purposes.
