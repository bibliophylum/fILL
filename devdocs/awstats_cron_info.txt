Building reports:
cd /usr/local/awstats/wwwroot/cgi-bin
perl awstats.pl -config=maplin3 -output -staticlinks >awstats.maplin3.html

A SIMPLE config file has been created: /etc/awstats/awstats.maplin3.conf
You should have a look inside to check and change manually main parameters.
You can then manually update your statistics for 'maplin3' with command:
> perl awstats.pl -update -config=maplin3
You can also read your statistics for 'maplin3' with URL:
> http://localhost/awstats/awstats.pl?config=maplin3
